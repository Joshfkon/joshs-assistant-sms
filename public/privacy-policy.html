Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2025

Program Name

Josh's Assistant SMS

What This Service Does

Josh's Assistant SMS is a personal, AI-powered text messaging service. When you send a text message to our phone number, you may receive an automated reply. The service is inbound-only: messages are only sent in direct response to messages you send first. No unsolicited or marketing messages are ever sent.

Information We Collect

When you text our number, the following information is processed:

  • Your phone number — provided by the carrier as part of the SMS delivery.
  • Message content — the text of the message you send.

Message content is passed to an AI service (Anthropic Claude) to generate a reply. Messages are not stored persistently by our application. We do not maintain conversation history or user profiles.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Receive your inbound text message.
  • Generate and deliver an automated reply.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your message content is sent to Anthropic (our AI provider) solely for the purpose of generating a response. Twilio is used as our messaging platform to send and receive SMS messages.

Data Retention

Our application does not store your messages or phone number. Standard server logs and Twilio message logs may retain data in accordance with their respective retention policies.

Opt-Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by simply not texting the number. Since this is an inbound-only service, no messages will be sent to you unless you text first. You may also text STOP to opt out.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, text HELP to our number or contact us at the support information provided in our Terms and Conditions.
